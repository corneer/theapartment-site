The magazine

74 pages of film, carefully put together. Every issue features an article series focusing on a specific topic, visually distinct from the rest of the issue. Combining up'n’coming illustrators and artists with a predefined set of tools, every issue gets its own touch.

FLM Weekend

Every thursday, a curated mix of news, in-depth articles and film tips is published as a digital publication. We use responsive cover images across social media, e-mails and digital publications to make it recognizable across different media.
